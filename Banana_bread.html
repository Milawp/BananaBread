The Best Banana Bread

Warm, Sweet, and Amazing Treat!


This delicious treat is an easy and quick recipe that is easy to impress guests with or comfort you on a cold day. It goes perfectly with a warm beverage, as a sweet breakfast, or a wonderful dessert. With a few quick and easy substitutions it can be made plant based and lactose free! Plant based alternatives
  • 1 cup White Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Butter *
  • 2 Eggs Beaten **
  • 3 Bananas (mashed)
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • Pinch of Salt
Mix in the order given. Bake in a Greased loaf pan at 350 ¬∞F (~175 ¬∞C) for 40 minutes to an hour.
Makes 2 small loafs * substitute butter for 2 TBSP of water & 6 TBSP of Coconut Oil (Or vegan butter)
** Substitude Egg for 1/2 cup of unsweetened applesauce or 6 TBSP of Aquafaba
